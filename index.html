Welcome to Arkadia

Arkadia is a theme park created as a destination for entertainment and experiences powered by blockchain technology. A sustainable sum-zero Metaverse which undertakes various gamified experiences for present or future NFT projects.

Main Road

Use your NFTs as Playable characters inside Arkadia and engage with multiple communities in the theme park. Arkadia's vision is to empower and uplift the NFT ecosystem by constructing a theme park suited to the story, background, and beliefs of various collections.

$PARK

The Arkadia Theme Park will be accessible via the Polygon Network. Arkadia's ingame currency is the $PARK token, which acts as the primary in-game currency in the park. The token serves multiple utilities in the park that are economically viable and have no effect on gameplay.

Partnerships

Forming strategic partnerships play a significant role in the creation of Arkadia. Primarily, these partnerships are formed with other NFT initiatives seeking to incorporate a gamified experience and join the ARKADIA mission. We also aim to conclude the project with internal and external partnerships that contribute to the brand awareness of the theme park.
